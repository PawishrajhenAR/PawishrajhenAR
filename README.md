## About

I'm a final-year B.Tech (Artificial Intelligence) student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology and Technical Lead at [KatalyzU](https://katalyzu.com), a digital studio shipping websites, business software, and AI products for real clients on fixed timelines.

I got into AI through computer vision, then RAG, then agents. Most of what I build now is agentic — systems that plan, call tools, and hand off to a UI people actually use. I still ship classical ML and full-stack apps when the problem needs it.

- **Production work:** multi-tenant billing backends, fuel-delivery operations platforms, client automations
- **Research:** IEEE-published co-author (adaptive AR for dementia care) and research lead on a hybrid ARIMA–LSTM ocean-forecasting paper
- **Comfort zone:** taking a messy prototype to something you'd hand to a client
